Tax Resources for Students

Tax laws can sometimes be confusing for those that have never had to deal with taxes before, especially for a new student who is coming to university directly from high school. Without the proper information it can be difficult to understand what you are obligated to report or what you have to pay. The intention then of this page is to help students gain access to the resources they need to get started in understanding tax in Canada. Below are a few links to different places in which you can find information about taxation in Canada as well as a few links to the appropiate forms.
